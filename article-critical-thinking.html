Critical Thinking in the Modern World

We live in an age where information spreads faster than ever before. Unfortunately, misinformation and false claims spread just as quickly.

Critical thinking helps people evaluate claims by asking important questions: What is the evidence? Is the source reliable? Can the claim be tested?

Without critical thinking, societies become vulnerable to propaganda, pseudoscience, and fear-based beliefs.

Teaching critical thinking is essential for scientific progress, democracy, and individual freedom.
