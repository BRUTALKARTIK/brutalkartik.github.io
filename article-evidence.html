Why Evidence Matters More Than Belief

Beliefs alone do not make something true. Many beliefs that were once widely accepted were later proven false when examined with evidence.

Evidence-based thinking allows us to correct mistakes, improve knowledge, and make better decisions as individuals and as a society.

Rationalism does not reject emotions or values. It simply insists that claims about reality must be supported by facts.

Science progresses not by faith, but by testing ideas against reality.
