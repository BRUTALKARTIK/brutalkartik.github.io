What Is Rationalism?

Rationalism is the practice of forming beliefs based on logic, evidence, and reason rather than superstition or blind authority.

Why Science Matters

Science is humanity’s most reliable method for understanding reality. It progresses by questioning, testing, and improving ideas.

Core Principles

  • Evidence over belief
  • Question everything
  • Extraordinary claims require extraordinary evidence
  • Change beliefs when facts change
